Our Story

55+ Years of Scottish Community

The Abu Dhabi Scottish Society has been a home away from home for Scots — and all friends of Scotland — since 1969. We are one of the oldest and most active expat societies in the UAE, fostering Scottish culture, friendships, and celebration across generations.

Our aim is to bring together Scottish people, their families, and everyone who shares a love of Scotland in Abu Dhabi. Whether you've roots in Inverness or just an affection for a good ceilidh, you belong here.
