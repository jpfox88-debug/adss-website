Our Story

Scotland's Spirit, Renewed

The Abu Dhabi Scottish Society has a proud history in the UAE — and in 2025, a passionate new committee breathed fresh life back into it. We're rebuilding a home away from home for Scots, their families, and all friends of Scotland in Abu Dhabi.

Our mission is simple: foster Scottish culture, build genuine friendships, and celebrate everything that makes Scotland great — right here in the heart of the Gulf. Whether you've roots in Inverness or just a love of a good ceilidh, you belong here.

Open to Scottish people, their families, and anyone with an interest in Scotland and Scottish culture. Membership gives you access to discounted event tickets, exclusive member benefits across Abu Dhabi, and a warm community that feels like home.

Open to All Friends of Scotland
You don't need to be Scottish to join. Whether you're a Scot far from home, a spouse, a friend, or simply someone who loves a good ceilidh — the Abu Dhabi Scottish Society welcomes you with open arms.
